VULCAN is the **photochemical kinetics module** of [PROTEUS](https://proteus-framework.org/PROTEUS) (/ˈproʊtiəs/, PROH-tee-əs), a modular Python framework for the coupled evolution of the atmospheres and interiors of rocky planets and exoplanets. See the [PROTEUS coupling page](Explanations/proteus_coupling.md) for an outline of how VULCAN is integrated into PROTEUS.
